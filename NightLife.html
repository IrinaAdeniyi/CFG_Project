When the sun sets, Romania brings out its outrageous nightlife fun and parties for night owls. Nightlife in Romania is vibrant with music for everyone and good prices. You can choose between proper night clubs and a more intimate atmosphere, where you can relax with your friends and enjoy live music or karaoke. Mojo, Coyote Cafe, Oldies, Yellow or Hard Rock Cafe are ideal places for live music and karaoke. If you prefer a proper night club a few good options would be: Le Gaga, Loft, BOA, Bamboo or Chaboo, where you can enjoy tematic parties, good champagne and the best music. Bucharest is the heart of the nightlife. Parties start around 10PM and don't normally end until morning. The clubs mentioned above are premium so they could be more on the high side regarding the prices, however most of the night clubs in the Old Town (Centrul Vechi) are really cheap. For example, the price of a beer varies between 10lei and 15 lei, which would mean about Â£2. Usually the entrance is free, unless there is a special event for which tickets would need to be acquired. Bucharest is defintely a city that knows how to party!
